nelangsa / ne · lang · sa /: sad
is an emotion that is characterized by feelings of lack, loss, and helplessness.
The inspiration to use the word miserable as the name of our project is because basically normal people generally only show their happy side to the outside world even though they are sad. Nelongso illustrates that sadness is a human thing, in this day and age many people are bullied because of crying or sadness. We want to show that sadness is natural and is needed by everyone. We also want to say that it's okay if you're sad and that there really is a time when you have to deal with sadness.


Nelangsa Project "is a growing creative team project with a sens of visual creativity. This project founded by friends who work closely as Photographers, we have the aim of building a creative visual, full of a sense of kinship between teams, and involving kindness. Our main strength are photo,focusing on personal moments, product branding, and nature photo."

"The visual style of Nelangsa is a play of color, set designs, stories in each photo, and a little fantasy."

"However, we are still excited to explore and learn about visual arts!"
